Introduction

Hello and welcome to The Old Man , a website created for the purpose of sharing the commentaries of Mr.P. He explains subject matters related to politics, current world events, religion, history and education but his views, sometimes hardline and controversial in this "modern" world require anonymity. And although Mr.P accepts that even the internet does'nt provide complete anonymity these days, it is surely a good stage for his privacy and anonymity to some extent.

About Mr.P

Mr.P, though young of age, has considerably old views on life(Old in the manner of it being shared by people from decades, sometimes centuries ago).
